Alexz Is A Portfolio, Business And Personal One Page/Multipurpose Html Template Built With Custom Opacity CSS. The Template Design Is Flat And Simple.This Template Gives Better Browsing And Great Experience.

Main features:
HTML5, CSS3 & pure js powered

Responsive Modern Design & Retina ready

Valid and Clean HTML5/CSS3.

Modern Slider and Carousel.

Directory structure
Skelet. comes with a directory structure that you can use for your project.

skelet/
├── css/
│   ├── skelet.css (CSS boilerplate)
│   └── app.css (is where all of your other CSS goes)
├── js/
│   ├── modules.js (is for all of your JS frameworks)
│   └── app.js (is where all of your other JS goes)
├── img/
│   ├── logo.png
│   └── touch.png
├── LICENSE
├── Readme.txt
└── index.html
